About Me

Hi, I'm John Stanley – a U.S. Air Force veteran with a strong background in aerospace maintenance and a growing passion for digital technology. I spent 8 years and 8 months serving as a Jet Engine Technician, where I developed a meticulous eye for detail, problem-solving skills, and a commitment to excellence.

After my military career, I transitioned into the tech world through a 3-year internship with Pratt & Whitney Canada. During this time, I worked on:

  • Web development projects improving internal tools and workflows
  • Custom applications built with C# and Unity
  • Lotus Notes database redevelopment
  • Modern SharePoint and WebCon form solutions
  • Supporting teams as a SharePoint Admin and Designer

Currently, I’m a F135 Technician at Pratt & Whitney, continuing my hands-on work in the aerospace field while expanding my expertise in technology and development.

In just a few weeks, I’ll be graduating with a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media and Web Design, combining my technical background with creative problem-solving skills to build meaningful, user-focused digital experiences.

My Projects

Understanding Search Engine Optimization Basics

This project explores the fundamentals of SEO and demonstrates how search engines operate through crawling, indexing, and ranking. It highlights the role of meta tags, focusing on keywords and descriptions, to improve search visibility and click-through rates. The project also explains how to create relevant content links using effective anchor text, maintaining contextual and destination relevance, and providing clear link purposes. Lastly, it emphasizes the importance of proper heading hierarchy to organize content logically, improve readability, and signal content importance to search engines, improving discoverability and user experience.

Responsive Design & Analytics

This project demonstrates the importance of responsive web design and preprocessing in modern web development. It highlights how responsive layouts enhance user experience by adapting to various screen sizes, improving accessibility, SEO rankings, and reducing bounce rates. The project also explores website analytics, focusing on key Google Analytics reports such as Audience, Acquisition, and Behavior, providing valuable insights into user demographics, traffic sources, and engagement. Additionally, it compares client-side and server-side preprocessing techniques, showcasing the benefits of tools like LESS and SASS for efficient styling, reusable code, and improved site performance.

This project showcases skills in 3D modeling, spatial design, audio integration, and interactive environment building for social virtual experiences.
